Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last Updated: March 2026

Your privacy matters to us. We will never sell your data — period. Read on to understand exactly what we collect and how it is used.

Who We Are

Carraway Business Group ("we," "us," or "our") is a business financing brokerage based in Arizona. We connect business owners with appropriate lending partners. We are not a lender. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information you provide to us through our website and contact form.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when completing our contact or pre-qualification form, including:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Business name and industry
  • Estimated monthly revenue and funding needs
  • Time in business
  • Any additional information you choose to share in free-text fields

We do not automatically collect sensitive financial account credentials, Social Security numbers, or tax identification numbers through this website.

How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used exclusively to:

  • Evaluate your business financing needs
  • Contact you to discuss potential funding options
  • Match you with lenders you explicitly choose to work with
  • Respond to your inquiries and follow up on your application

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or business information to any third party — ever.

Your information is only shared with lending partners in the following circumstance:

  • You have reviewed the lender and explicitly authorized us to submit your information to them

We will always discuss lending options with you first and obtain your approval before sharing any of your details with a lender.

Data Security

We take reasonable and industry-standard steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse, including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption on our website
  • Limiting access to your data to authorized personnel only
  • Storing data on secured systems

While no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, we are committed to protecting your information using best practices.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by applicable law. If you would like your information removed from our records, please contact us and we will honor that request promptly.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Withdraw consent for us to contact you at any time

Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use basic cookies and analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our site. These tools collect anonymized data and do not identify you personally. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies independently.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our website after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us
